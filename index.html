About
Kevin J
Smithson

Acrylic Oil Landscape Auckland

Vibrant original acrylic and oil landscapes created by New Zealand artist Kevin J Smithson. Kevin's paintings explore the connection between land and water as well as sense of place, using his expressive traditional style of artwork.

Kevin believes art should be approachable and accessible to everyone.

Located in Auckland, New Zealand, Kevin continues in the artistic tradition of his father, grandfather and other ancestors who have passed down their skills as portrait and landscape artists since the early 1900s.

Current artworks are available from the Italian Lakes and Southern Landscapes series. Kevin welcomes enquiries for the sale of his portfolio pieces as well as potential commissions of a custom piece within your preferred size and budget.
